I Cannot Log In?

  1. If you see an error message such as Encountered an Error: Try clearing your cache; Email an administrator if you are unsure of how to do this
  2. If you see the error message Invalid Username and/or Password: This means you are either not set up on Lead Connect or are incorrectly setup. Contact the Group BDC admin who will check this for you.
  3. Any other issues with logging in: Contact your admin. If they are not availible email either Lead Connect at: leadconnect@vauxhall.co.uk (Open Monday - Friday 8.30 - 5.30) or the DAC at: DAC.eur.english@gm.com

How do I set up a user on Lead Connect?

  1. Contact the Group BDC Admin, they will set this up for you
  2. If the Group BDC Admin is unavailable email either Lead Connect at: leadconnect@vauxhall.co.uk (Open Monday - Friday 8.30 - 5.30) or the DAC at: DAC.eur.english@gm.com

Missing Opportunities?

  1. If you are unable to see an opportunity then this may be because it hasnt been opened within 48 hours of it being assigned. These opportunities are sent to the Vauxhall Contact Center, they contact the customer and appologise for the delay in contacting them and ask if they are still happy to go to the same dealer, or if they would prefer to go to another dealership (where appropriate dealers are located close to them)

No Trackback Numbers?

IF the opportunity does not come with a Trackback number attached to it there could be a number of reasons for this

  1. Normal number provided: Contact the customer through this number provided and manually mark the lead as contacted, using the Feedback tab. Then contact either the Group BDC Admin or the Retailer Sales Admin who will look in this further
  2. No number at all: Contact either the Group BDC Admin or Retailer Sales Admin
  3. Anything else; Contact the Group BDC Admin

If you have any other questins that have have not be answered above contact the Group BDC Admin, who will investigate the issue further Send Mail
