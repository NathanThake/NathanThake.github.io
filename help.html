How do I set up a user on Lead Connect?

Contact the Group BDC Admin, they will set this up for you

If the Group BDC Admin is unavailable email either Lead Connect or the DAC

I Cannot Log In?

If you see an error message such as Encountered an Error: Try clearing your cache; Email an administrator if you are unsure of how to do this

If you see the error message Invalid Username and/or Password: This means you are either not set up on Lead Connect or are incorrectly setup. Contact the Group BDC admin who will check this for you.

Any other issues with logging in: Contact your admin.

Missing Opportunities?

If you are unable to see an opportunity then this may be because it hasnt been opened within 48 hours of it being assigned. These opportunities are sent to the Vauxhall Contact Center, they contact the customer and appologise for the delay in contacting them and ask if they are still happy to go to the same dealer, or if they would prefer to go to another dealership (where appropriate dealers are located close to them)

No Trackback Numbers?

If the opportunity does not come with a Trackback number attached to it there could be a number of reasons for this

Normal number provided: Contact the customer through this number provided and manually mark the lead as contacted, using the Feedback tab.

No number at all: Contact either the Group BDC Admin or Retailer Sales Admin

Others?

If you have any other questins that have have not be answered above contact the Group BDC Admin using the form below, who will investigate the issue further
