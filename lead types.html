Lead Types

The Different Lead Groups and Types In Lead Connect

There are a number of different lead groups within Lead Connect, and within these groups there are a number of different types of leads as well.

Lets take a look at these in more detail

New Vehicle Sales

As the name suggests these leads are all about New Vehicle Enquires from Adam to Zafira, any lead that has the focus of selling a New Vehicle ends up here.

All leads that come under the New Vehicle Sale Category are Margin leads, and count towards our sales conversion KPI

Within this group there are a vast number of lead types, coming for a number of different sources. Common lead types include.

Internet Quotes

They can come either from our Retailer website, Vauxhall home site, or any other side advertising Vauxhall. A heand raiser lead, the customer wants to get an idea of the cost and options available.

Test Drive

Can come from a number of different sources - some will come with a time and date that the customer would like to complete a test drive on.

Brouchure Requests

These Customers are looking for more information, sending a brochur is good. But how about they come in and see us? We can answer more questions easier this way.

Website Vouchers

Where a customer has filled in their information to recieve a voucher for moeny off the price of a New Vehicle. These leads are considered HOT Leads.

Vehicle Valuation

Where a customer has been looking on the webiste at a new vehicle and has request a valuation for their current vehicle against this.

Partners Registration

When a customer registers themselves onto the partners scheme.

Used Vehicle Sales

As the name suggests these leads are all about Used Vehicle Enquires, any lead that has the focus of selling a Used Vehicle ends up here.

All leads that comes under the Used Vehicle Sales category are Non-Margin leads.

Within this group there are a vast number of lead types, coming from a number of different sources. Common lead types include.

Network Q Sales Enquiry

These come through from Network Q on specific used vehicles, often wanting quotes for part exchanges against vehicles

Used Vehicle Enquiry

These come from our own website, normally asking for quotes or test drives

Vehicle Vauluation

Again these come from our website, where the customer is requesting a part-exchange quote against a used vehicle

Motability

Once again as the name suggests these leads are all about Motability enquiries.

All Leads under the Motability catagory are Non-Margin leads.

Common Lead types include

Contact Expiry

These are automatically uploaded from Vauxhall when the customers existing Motability contract is coming to an end.

Test Drive

Where a customer who is on the Motability scheme has contact Vauxhall and requested a test drive to decide their next vehicle.

General Enquiry

These are general enquires from our website. These leads do not always relate to sales so should always be checked and passed on as appropriate.

All General Enquiry leads are Non-Margin leads.

Finance-Gmac

These leads are uploaded by GMAC (Vauxhall Finance) when the customers exisitng finance agreement is either close to ending, or if the customer is in a position whereby they could potentually change the vehicle

All Finance-Gmac leads are Conversion leads, and count towards our sales conversion KPI

Common lead types include

Contract Expiry

Where a customers exisiting finance agreement is coming to a close

Sales Driver

Where the customer should be in a parity situation

Vauxhall Finance Equity Program

where extra support is offered to select customers in order to change their vehicle

Sales Driver Hot

Where the customer has requested a settlement figure and also has asked to be contacted
