The Dashboard

The dashboard is your basecamp in lead connect. Here you can see all New, Open, Closed and the Total Number of leads within the timeframe specified on the left hand side.

To go further into the lead details simply click on the blue number in the relevant box for the lead type.

Lead Types

There are various different lead types within lead connect.

The Major groups are

  • New Vehicle Sales
  • Used Vehicle sales
  • Finance-GMAC
  • Motability

Time Periods

All Leads apart from finance leads must be actioned and called within 1 hour of being assigned.

Finance leads must be called the same day.

More Details

To view all leads that are currently open, click the oportunities tab next to the dashboard tab. Then on the drop down menu to the right hand side select the following option: 07 - Sales New and Open.

Exploring The Larger Opportunities Window

To access the larger oportunites window, click on the blue lead ID number

There are four columns on the larger opportnties tab.

The customer column: Displays all known customer details as well as trackback numbers.

The Opportunity column: Diplays all details about the opportunity type.

The Vehicle Information collum: Displays all information about the enquiry vehicle.

The Status and Deadline column: Displays relevent informtion on the KPI status of the lead.
