Best Practice Procedures

There are a number of best practice procedures relating to Lead Connect, these have been agreed by Platinum in conjunction with Vauxhall.

Lead Process

A detailed process regaridng how leads are dealt with when they come in and how they should be progressed. Including when the main person responsible for Lead Connect is out of the office.

Contact Times

A detailed process showing times to contact leads based on

  • The Time The Lead Was Assinged
  • The Lead Type
  • The Time The Lead Was Last Tried
  • Times Best Proven To Contact Customers

Full Copies of these Sheets are availible from your Sales Manager

FeedBack

New customers must ALWAYS be loaded into Dealerweb, with the source being set to Lead Connect.

If a customer is already in Dealerweb and is not an already ongoing, a new lead must be started for this customer. Once again with the source of the lead being set to Lead Connect.

ALL activites regarding lead connect MUST be recorded into Dealerweb.

Emailing Leads

Lead should only be emailed to for the following reasons

  • If More than THREE attempts to contact the customer have been unsuccessful
  • IF the contact number for the customer is incorrect
  • IF the customer asks to be emailed rather than to be called
